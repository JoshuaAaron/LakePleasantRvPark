Welcome

Lake Pleasant RV Park is a family-owned and operated RV Park for over 30 years. With commitment to quality and excellence, it is our mission to provide a safe, beautiful and well-maintained property for our long-term residents, seasonal visitors and short-term guests. It is our duty to provide unparalleled customer service combined with the finest offering of RV resorts, amenities, and activities for you and your family to enjoy the great outdoors of western Washington.

Our lovely park includes a wildlife habitat and small lake filled with fish and waterfowl. All sites are within a short walking distance to the small lake and many are waterfront. Watch the ducks and cranes, listen to the bullfrogs and enjoy our lawns and trees. We're the perfect spot to jump off to wine tasting and dining in Woodinville or exploring Seattle attractions and museums. We have wooded nature trails, a putting/chipping green, and access to paved bike trails that lead to downtown Seattle. Explore the Northwest then come home to a greenbelt haven. We do fill up in summer, so please book your site early. We welcome all rigs - big or small.
